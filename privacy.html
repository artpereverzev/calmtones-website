Information We Collect

Calm Tones is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Audio Processing

All audio processing happens locally on your device. No audio data is sent to external servers.

App Usage

We do not track your usage patterns, listening habits, or any personal preferences.

Live Activities

Live Activities data remains on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Location Services

If you choose to enable location-based features, your location data is processed locally on your device to provide personalized sound recommendations. This location information is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All app settings, preferences, and user data are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. This data is never synchronized with external servers unless you explicitly choose to use cloud backup features provided by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Calm Tones does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising services. We do not use any tracking technologies or cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@calmtones.app
